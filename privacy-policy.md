# Privacy Policy

MBTA Widgets respects your privacy. Puneet Ramini has developed this mobile application

## Information Collection
This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## App Functionality
The app allows users to select and save transit routes and stops locally on their device. This data is not transmitted or stored externally. User preferences such as favorite stops and widget settings are stored locally on the device and are not transmitted to any server.

## Third-Party Services
The app uses the MBTA public API to fetch real-time transit data. No personal information is sent to MBTA or any third party.

## Data Storage
All preferences (such as selected routes and widget settings) are stored locally on your device.

## Analytics and Tracking
This app does not use analytics, tracking tools, or advertising services.

## Changes to This Policy
This policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page.
